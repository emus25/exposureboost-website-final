FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need a phone with NFC?
Almost every phone from the last few years has NFC. For older phones, your card also has a QR code so anyone can scan it.
Can I change my details later?
Yes — update anything from your dashboard and every card you've shared updates instantly. Profile updates are free forever.
Is my data secure?
You control exactly what's shown and can change it anytime. We never sell your data.
What if I lose my card?
Deactivate it from your dashboard and order a replacement linked to the same profile — your details stay the same.
Do you ship internationally?
UK delivery is free. We ship worldwide — international rates are shown at checkout.
